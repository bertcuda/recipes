Ingredients:
 - 1 can peeled Marzano tomatoes
 - 2 tbsp olive oil
 - 2 cloves garlic
 - salt and pepper to taste
 - chopped parsley

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, add olive oil and garlic.
  2. Cook over medium heat until garlic begins to brown.
  3. Remove garlic.
  4. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Add chopped parsley, cook for another 2-3 minutes and serve over pasta.
